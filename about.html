Who We Are

Rooted in passion

The Grounded Way Café was born in 2019 from a single stubborn belief: that a café should feel like the best version of staying home. Founder Mireille Dupont had spent a decade working as a florist in Paris before returning to Montreal with an obsession for slow mornings and beautiful things.

She combined her love of botanical design with her partner Éric's lifelong passion for independent literature, and the result was something Montreal had never quite seen before — a place where you could buy a wedding bouquet, discover a favourite novel, and linger over a perfectly made cortado, all in the same visit.

Today we are a team of nine dreamers, each one bringing their own art to our shared space. The flowers change with the season. The book tables are rearranged every Sunday. The espresso is always freshly ground.

What We Believe

Our values

🌿
Slow & Intentional

We resist the rush. Every arrangement is made by hand. Every book is chosen with purpose. We believe the best things take time.

🌱
Sustainably Sourced

Our flowers come from local and ethical growers. Our coffee is direct-trade. Our packaging is plastic-free. We care about where things come from.

Community First

We stock local authors, host community readings, and collaborate with neighbourhood artists. This space belongs to the people who fill it.
