Common Questions

Before you visit

Do you take walk-ins for custom arrangements?

Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome for ready-made and seasonal bouquets. For bespoke or event arrangements, we recommend booking a consultation at least one week in advance.

Can I bring my dog?

Absolutely — well-behaved dogs on leashes are warmly welcomed on our terrace. We even keep a water bowl out front. Indoor visits are at the barista's discretion.

Do you ship flowers or books?

We offer local same-day delivery within Montreal (Island) for flower orders placed before 11am. Book orders ship Canada-wide via Canada Post within 3–5 business days.

Is the café accessible?

Yes. Our front entrance is step-free, and we have an accessible washroom on the ground floor. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you need specific accommodations.

Do you have WiFi?

We do — though we gently encourage you to put the phone down and pick up a book instead. The password is on the chalkboard at the counter.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We host private book clubs, floral workshops, and after-hours buyouts for small gatherings. Contact us to enquire about availability and pricing.
